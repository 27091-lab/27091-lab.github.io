How It Works

Companies must implement detection systems and transparency tools. AI systems will be classified by risk level, with stricter rules applied to systems that affect public safety or democracy.

Enforcement

Violations will result in fines and restrictions. A new oversight agency will monitor AI systems and ensure compliance.
